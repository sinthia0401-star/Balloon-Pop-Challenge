🎪 How To Play

Objective

Pop as many balloons as possible before the 30-second timer runs out, without letting too many float away unpopped.

How to Play

  • Choose a difficulty level, then tap Start Game.
  • Balloons rise up from the bottom of the game area at random positions, colors, and sizes.
  • Click or tap a balloon to pop it before it floats off the top of the screen.
  • Use Pause / Resume anytime to take a break — the timer and balloons freeze.
  • Use Restart to reset the current round back to the difficulty screen.
  • The round ends automatically when the timer hits 0 or when you run out of lives.

Difficulty Levels

Easy — balloons rise slowly and appear less often. Great for warming up.

Medium — moderate rise speed and a faster spawn rate. A balanced challenge.

Hard — balloons rise quickly and spawn rapidly. Only for quick reflexes!

Scoring

  • Popping a balloon: +1 point.
  • Missing a balloon (letting it float off the top): −1 life.
  • Losing all 3 lives ends the game immediately, even if time remains.

High Score

Your best score is automatically saved in your browser's local storage, so it will still be there the next time you visit — even after refreshing the page. It's stored only on this device/browser.
